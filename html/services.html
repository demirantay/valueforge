Comprehensive Handyman Services

At Handyman Pratt, we offer a wide range of handyman services to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our services include:

Home Repairs

At Handyman Pratt, we know that keeping up with general home maintenance can be a daunting task. Our comprehensive general maintenance services are designed to address all those small but crucial tasks that keep your home running smoothly and looking its best. Our services include:

  • Routine Inspections: Regular checks of your home’s infrastructure to identify and fix potential issues before they become major problems.
  • Filter Replacements: Replacing air filters in HVAC systems to ensure clean air and efficient operation.
  • Gutter Cleaning: Clearing debris from gutters and downspouts to prevent water damage and ensure proper drainage.
  • Minor Plumbing Repairs: Fixing leaks, replacing washers, and ensuring all plumbing fixtures are in good working order.
  • Window and Door Maintenance: Inspecting and repairing seals, hinges, and locks to improve energy efficiency and security.
  • Caulking and Sealing: Re-caulking bathrooms, kitchens, and other areas to prevent water damage and maintain a polished look.
  • Fixture Updates: Replacing outdated or faulty fixtures like faucets, showerheads, and light fixtures.
  • Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Detector Checks: Testing and replacing batteries to ensure your home’s safety systems are operational.
  • Pest Prevention: Sealing entry points and advising on measures to keep pests at bay.
  • General Upkeep: Addressing miscellaneous tasks such as tightening loose screws, lubricating squeaky hinges, and touching up paint.

Plumbing Work

At Handyman Pratt, we understand that plumbing issues can cause significant inconvenience and potential damage to your home. Our comprehensive plumbing services are designed to address a wide range of needs, ensuring your plumbing systems function efficiently and reliably. Our services include:

  • Leak Repairs: Identifying and fixing leaks in pipes, faucets, and fixtures to prevent water wastage and damage.
  • Drain Cleaning: Clearing clogged drains and pipes to ensure smooth water flow and prevent backups.
  • Fixture Installation and Repair: Installing and repairing sinks, faucets, showerheads, toilets, and other plumbing fixtures.
  • Pipe Inspections and Repairs: Conducting thorough inspections to detect any issues in your plumbing system and performing necessary repairs.
  • Water Heater Services: Installing, maintaining, and repairing water heaters to ensure a consistent supply of hot water.
  • Garbage Disposal Repair and Installation: Fixing or installing garbage disposals to help manage kitchen waste efficiently.
  • Sump Pump Maintenance: Inspecting, repairing, and replacing sump pumps to prevent basement flooding.
  • Toilet Repairs: Fixing running toilets, replacing broken parts, and addressing flushing issues.
  • Emergency Plumbing Services: Providing prompt and reliable service for urgent plumbing issues to minimize damage and inconvenience.

Electrical Work

At Handyman Pratt, we recognize the importance of a safe and efficient electrical system in your home. Our comprehensive electrical services are designed to handle a wide range of needs, ensuring your home's electrical systems operate smoothly and safely. Our services include:

  • Lighting Installation and Repair: Installing and repairing indoor and outdoor lighting fixtures, including ceiling lights, chandeliers, recessed lighting, and landscape lighting.
  • Outlet and Switch Installation: Adding new outlets and switches, upgrading existing ones, and fixing faulty or outdated components.
  • Ceiling Fan Installation: Installing and repairing ceiling fans to improve air circulation and comfort in your home.
  • Electrical Panel Upgrades: Upgrading your electrical panel to handle increased power demands and enhance the safety of your electrical system.
  • Circuit Breaker Replacement: Replacing old or faulty circuit breakers to prevent electrical overloads and ensure reliable power distribution.
  • Wiring and Rewiring: Installing new wiring for home additions or remodeling projects and replacing outdated or damaged wiring.
  • Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Detector Installation: Installing and testing smoke and carbon monoxide detectors to ensure your home’s safety systems are operational.
  • Surge Protection: Installing whole-house surge protectors to safeguard your appliances and electronics from power surges.
  • Troubleshooting and Repairs: Diagnosing and fixing electrical issues, such as flickering lights, tripped breakers, and faulty wiring.
  • Home Automation: Installing smart home devices and systems, including smart thermostats, lighting controls, and security systems.

Painting & Drywall

At Handyman Pratt, we understand that fresh paint and well-maintained drywall can significantly enhance the appearance and value of your home. Our comprehensive painting and drywall services are designed to give your home a polished, professional finish. Our services include:

  • Interior Painting: Expertly painting walls, ceilings, trim, and doors to give your home a fresh and updated look. We use high-quality paints and professional techniques to ensure a smooth, even finish.
  • Exterior Painting: Protecting and beautifying your home's exterior with durable paint that can withstand the elements. We handle everything from surface preparation to final touches.
  • Drywall Installation: Installing new drywall for home additions, remodels, or repairs. Our team ensures precise measurements and secure installations for a seamless look.
  • Drywall Repair: Patching holes, fixing cracks, and repairing water-damaged drywall to restore your walls to their original condition. We match textures and finishes to blend repairs seamlessly.
  • Texturing and Finishing: Applying various textures and finishes to walls and ceilings, including smooth, knockdown, orange peel, and more, to achieve the desired aesthetic.
  • Wallpaper Removal: Safely and efficiently removing old wallpaper and preparing the surface for a fresh coat of paint or new wall covering.
  • Popcorn Ceiling Removal: Removing outdated popcorn ceilings and applying a modern, smooth finish to update the look of your home.
  • Color Consultation: Offering professional advice on color selection and combinations to help you achieve the perfect look for your space.
  • Trim and Molding Painting: Carefully painting baseboards, crown molding, and other trim for a clean and refined appearance.
  • Cabinet Refinishing: Refreshing the look of your kitchen or bathroom cabinets with a new coat of paint or stain.

Carpentry

At Handyman Pratt, we understand the importance of quality carpentry in creating a functional and aesthetically pleasing home. Our comprehensive carpentry services are designed to address a wide range of needs, from custom woodworking to repairs and installations. Our services include:

  • Custom Woodworking: Designing and building custom furniture, cabinetry, shelves, and more to suit your unique style and requirements.
  • Trim and Molding Installation: Installing baseboards, crown molding, wainscoting, and other decorative trim to enhance the beauty and character of your home.
  • Door and Window Installation: Installing and repairing interior and exterior doors and windows, including framing, trimming, and weatherproofing to ensure proper fit and function.
  • Deck and Patio Construction: Building and repairing decks, patios, pergolas, and other outdoor structures to expand your living space and enhance your outdoor experience.
  • Framing: Constructing and repairing the structural framework of your home, including walls, floors, and roofs, to ensure stability and durability.
  • Flooring Installation and Repair: Installing and repairing hardwood, laminate, and engineered wood flooring to add beauty and value to your home.
  • Cabinet Installation and Repair: Installing and repairing kitchen and bathroom cabinets, including custom and prefabricated options, to improve storage and functionality.
  • Staircase Construction and Repair: Building and repairing staircases, including handrails and balusters, to ensure safety and aesthetic appeal.
  • Furniture Assembly and Repair: Assembling and repairing furniture, including ready-to-assemble and custom pieces, to save you time and effort.
  • Built-Ins and Shelving: Designing and installing built-in bookcases, entertainment centers, and custom shelving solutions to maximize space and organization.
