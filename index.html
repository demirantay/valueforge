Accessiblity is the act of making sure your website is usable by everyone. Even those with disabilities who have to use a screen reader or have colour-blindedness. Did you know that lack of care to website accessibility cost Domino's Pizza millions in fighting a web accessibility lawsuit?

Many agencies claim to create accessible websites, but these fail a simple screen reader test. We don't do that. All websites include a dark mode and are tested with screen readers, colour-blind emulators, link-checkers, and other assistive technologies to ensure compliance with WCAGs 2.2 guidelines (Level AA). That's one less worry for you to think about.
